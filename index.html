The Castles of Britain

Welcome to British Castles. Your first question may very well be "What is a Castle?" Well a castle is defined as a fortified structure built in and around the Middle Ages. They were often built by people of nobility or royalty and by militaries alike. These Magnificent structures played as homes to Kings, Queens and Nobility, The designs of these overwhelming castles play a large part in the shaping of Britain and the places surrounding them, as they were designed to impose strength and authority over their dominion and served as a key to any rulers power.

These fortresses also served sometimes more sinister roles, for tyrants to hold a vice like grip over the kingdom. Often they were prisons to their enemies and were often places of unspeakable torture. These castles are hundreds of years old and the were build to last, and for the most part most are still standing till this very day. We will delve into the size and age of these castles and the roles they played in the relms of the time.

This website is designed to beguile the reader as we get to see these amazing strongholds of Britain and some of the history evolved around them. Here we encourage you to learn more about these places for yourselves and we hope that this website serves as a starting point for you to garner a greater intrest in these wonderful buidings of old. So wait no longer and delve into what is know as some of the most beautiful and yet truly terrifying places in of the old age...
